In today's fast-paced digital workspace, converting files between PDF, Word, and PowerPoint is a daily necessity. However, a hidden security threat lies in the convenience of "free online converters."

When you upload a confidential financial report or a client contract to an unverified third-party tool, you are handing over your data to an unknown server. Many of these free platforms make their money by harvesting data from the documents you upload.

### The Risks of Unsecured Conversion
1. Data Breaches: Sensitive client information can be intercepted or stored indefinitely on unsecured servers.
2. Malware Injection: Malicious sites can inject macro-viruses into the converted files they return to you.
3. Compliance Violations: Uploading customer data to unauthorized tools violates GDPR and other privacy regulations.
